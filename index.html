About Me

I am in my final year of a BSc (Hons.) Software Engineering program at NSBM Green University, affiliated with the University of Plymouth, UK. My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in software development principles, with a particular focus on mobile application development and system automation.

Throughout my studies, I've developed strong technical skills in mobile application development using the Flutter framework, front-end web development with ReactJS and NextJS, and backend integration with Firebase. One of my key projects is "BalangodaPULSE", a system designed to automate and modernize the Urban Council Balangoda's processes.

I'm passionate about creating impactful software solutions and am currently seeking an internship to gain hands-on experience and further develop my skills withing a dynamic team environment.
